1. **initPopupPreviewToggle()** method (lines 343-353):
   - Listens for changes to #bwg-igf-popup-enabled checkbox
   - Triggers updatePreview() when checkbox state changes

2. **getFormSettings()** method (line 984):
   - Captures popupEnabled state: `$('#bwg-igf-popup-enabled').is(':checked')`

3. **updatePreview()** method (lines 937-945):
   - Applies cursor style based on popup state:
     - When `settings.popupEnabled === true`: applies `cursor: pointer`
     - When `settings.popupEnabled === false`: applies `cursor: default`

**User Experience:**

- **Default cursor** indicates items are non-interactive (no popup)

This helps users understand the effect of the popup setting immediately in the preview.
